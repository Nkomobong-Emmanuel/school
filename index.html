Financial Assistance

A limited number of means-tested financial awards worth, in some cases, up to 100 per cent of day fees, can be applied for through the Headington Access Programme (HAP) on application to the School for Year 7 (11+). Additional means-tested financial awards of up to 50% of the fees are also available at Year 7 (11+) and Year 9 (13+). Financial assistance may also considered for Year 12 (16+) through our Special Awards Programme. Please read our brochure for more information. If parents believe they may be eligible to receive financial assistance with senior school day fees, they should contact the Registrar for advice via admissions@headington.org. Exceptionally, under some circumstances, the application fee and deposit may be waived if this would cause your family financial hardship. We regret we are unable to offer financial assistance for boarding places or for the Prep School. All information about bursary applications is kept strictly confidential.

How do I get Admitted into the school?

Our main entry points are at Reception, Year 3 and Year 5 in the Prep School and Year 7 (Upper 3), Year 9 (Upper 4) and Year 12 (Lower 6) in the Senior School but we welcome applications at many different entry points depending on the availability of places. The process and requirements will vary depending on your child's age.
